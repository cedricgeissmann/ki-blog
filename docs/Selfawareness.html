Self-awareness

Once theory of mind can be established, sometime well into the future of AI, the final step will be for AI to become self-aware. This kind of AI prossesses human-level consciousness and understands its own existence in the world, as well as the presence and emotion state of others. It would be able to understand what others may need based on not just what they commumicate to them but how they communicate it. the self-awareness in AI relies both on human researchers understanding the premise of consciousness and then learning how to peplicate that so it can be built into machines.

Will in the future human researchers find out how to make the theory of mind or even how AI to becomes self-aware?
