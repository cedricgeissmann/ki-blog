Theory of mind

theory of mind is just theoretical. It is not yet achieved the technological and scientific capabilities necessary to reach this next level of AI. The concept is based on the pschological premise of understanding that other living things have thoughts and emotions that affect the behavior of one's self. In terms of AI machines, this would mean that AI could comprehend how humans, animals, living beins and other machines feel and make decisions through self-reflection and determination and the utilize that information to make decisions of their own. Essentially, machines would have to be able to grasp and process the concept of "mind", the fluctautions of emotions in decision-making and a litany of other psychological concepts in real time, creating a two-way relationship between people and AI.
